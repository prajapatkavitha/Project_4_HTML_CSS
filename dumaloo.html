Dum Aloo Recipe

Ingredients :

  1. 500 grams Baby Potatoes
  2. 2 large Onions, finely chopped
  3. 3 medium Tomatoes, pureed
  4. 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  5. 2 Green Chilies, chopped
  6. 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  7. 1 Bay Leaf
  8. 2-3 Cloves
  9. 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  10. 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  11. 1 teaspoon Red Chili Powder
  12. 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  13. 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  14. 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
  15. 2 tablespoons Fresh Cream (optional)
  16. Oil, for frying + 2 tablespoons
  17. Salt, to taste
  18. Water, as needed
  19. Fresh Coriander Leaves, for garnish

Preparation Process :

  1. Prepare Potatoes: Boil the baby potatoes until tender.
    Peel them and prick them all over with a fork.
    Heat oil in a deep frying pan and fry the potatoes until golden brown.
    Remove and set aside.
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan.
    Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cinnamon stick.
    Sauté for a few seconds. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
    Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt.
    Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Combine Potatoes and Masala: Add the fried potatoes to the masala.
    Mix well and add water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
    Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Finish and Garnish: Add garam masala and kasuri methi. Mix well.
    Optional: Add fresh cream for a richer flavor. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
