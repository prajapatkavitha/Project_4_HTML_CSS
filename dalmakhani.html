Dal Makahani Recipe

Ingredients :

  1. Black Lentils (Urad Dal)
  2. Red Kidney Beans
  3. Butter & Cream
  4. Onion
  5. Spices : ginger
    garam masala
    cumin
    red chilli powder
    salt & pepper
  6. Tomatoes

Preparation Process :

  1. Soaking the dries pulsed overnight to rehydrate the dried lentils and kidney beans.
  2. Then we are going to slow cook the lentils and beans until they are lovely and tender,
    then mash some of them so you have a slightly smoother texture.
  3. Fry onion in ghee and butter until soft.
  4. Build up the flavour with spices and tomatoes to give a delicious aromatic base.
  5. Then add the cooked lentils and kidney beans to the pan.
    The dal is simmered for an hour, allowing the flavours to combine and the dal to thicken.
  6. Add cream and a bit more butter for a lovely smooth rich finish, then serving!
