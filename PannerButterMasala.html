Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients :

  1. 200 grams paneer (cottage cheese)
  2. 3 onions
  3. 3 tomatoes
  4. 10 Cashew nuts
  5. 1 inch ginger
  6. 4 - 5 pods garlic
  7. 1/2 tablespoon coriander powder
  8. 1 tablespoon kashmiri chili powder
  9. 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  10. 1 bay leaf
  11. 3 cloves
  12. 3 cardamom
  13. 1 inch cinnamon
  14. 1 teaspoon kasuri methi leaves (dry fenugreek)
  15. 2 - 3 sprigs coriander leaves
  16. To taste salt
  17. 2 tablespoons butter
  18. 1 1/2 tablespoons fresh cream

Preparation Process :

  1. Finely chop the onions and tomatoes.Soak the cashew nuts in water.
    Heat some oil in a pan, and add the chopped
  2. TCook till they are tender. As soon as the onions turn translucent, add the chopped tomatoes.
    Add salt, and mix well.
  3. Close the lid, and cook for 3 minutes on a low flame.
    Open the lid, and add coriander powder, chili powder, garam masala, and cashew nuts.
  4. Stir well, cook till the juices start to disappear, and switch off. Let it cool.
    Transfer the onion and tomato mixture to a blender, add a cup of water, and grind to a fine paste.
  5. This is the "masala" of our paneer butter masala. Add more water if the masala it's too thick.
    Heat a pan, and melt the butter. Fry the dry spices, bay leaf,
    cardamom, cloves and cinnamon, for 1 minute on a low flame.
  6. Crush the ginger and garlic, and add to the pan.
    Add the masala to the pan, add more chili powder (if required) for the colour, and mix well.
    Cook with the lid closed for 3 minutes.
  7. Open the lid, and cook for some more time if you want to reduce the gravy.
    Add the paneer cubes and the Kasuri methi leaves.
  8. Add some cream and mix well.
    Garnish with coriander leaves and a pinch of methi leaves. Serve hot with roti or naan
