Rajma Curry Recipe

Ingredients :

    <
  1. 1 cup Red Kidney Beans (Rajma), soaked overnight
  2. 2 medium Onions, finely chopped
  3. 3 medium Tomatoes, pureed
  4. 1 tablespoon Ginger-Garlic Paste
  5. 2 Green Chilies, chopped
  6. 1 teaspoon Cumin Seeds
  7. 1 Bay Leaf
  8. 1 inch Cinnamon Stick
  9. 2-3 Cloves
  10. 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric Powder
  11. 1 teaspoon Red Chili Powder
  12. 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder
  13. 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  14. 1/2 teaspoon Cumin Powder
  15. Salt, to taste
  16. 3 tablespoons Oil or Ghee
  17. Fresh Coriander Leaves, for garnish
  18. Water, as needed

Preparation Process :

  1. Rajma: Pressure cook the soaked rajma with water and
    a pinch of salt until soft (about 4-5 whistles). Set aside.
  2. Prepare Masala: Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
    Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, and cloves.
    Sauté for a few seconds. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
    Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
    Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
    Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder.
    Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Combine Rajma and Masala: Add the cooked rajma along with its water to the masala.
    Mix well. Add additional water as needed to achieve the desired consistency.
    Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Finish and Garnish: Add garam masala and adjust salt.
    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  6. Serve: Serve hot with rice or roti.
